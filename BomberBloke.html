About the project

This project was part of the Games Technology Research Project module. As it was my dissertaion I wanted to explore the concept of procedural content generation, more specifically cyclic generation. This is due to my knowledge around PCG being very limited and the use of PCG in games allow games to have a different approach to their world building and content creation. When I found about an upcoming PCG method called Cyclic which was being developed by Joris Dormans I wanted to explore its concepts and its validity in being used as a PCG method, specifically for roguelike games since that is the original deployment of PCG in games. The outcome of this project was me creating grammar rules to simulate a cyclic generator to create a dungeon layout.
