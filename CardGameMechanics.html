Card Game Mechanics

This post covers a Game development course project made in Unity with C#.

This game focuses on card game mechanics, drawing inspiration from Hearthstone and Inscryption. The player plays against an AI, with the winning being decided by a scale system as seen in Inscryption. This mechanics works with each side having to deal a certain amount of damage to fully tip the scale in their favour. With each damage representing one point to tip the scale. The player has 4 lanes to attack/defend in.

Game Features

  • Scene loading
  • 4 lane card battling
  • Mana based card placing
  • AI
  • Turn based combat
  • Point based winning system
